What is Pig Latin?

Pig Latin originates from Piglatinia, a secret land of word-flippers. It’s now a playful code in America, turning English words into fun phrases. A popular rumor says Thomas Jefferson used it, though this has never been confirmed!

How to Translate:

  • If a word starts with a vowel, add "yay" or "way" depending on which region of Piglatinia you come from (West Coast or East Coast). (e.g. "apple" → "appleyay" or "appleway")
  • If a word starts with a consonant or consonant cluster, move it to the end and add "ay". (e.g. "hello" → "ellohay")
  • Punctuation and capitalization stay the same.
