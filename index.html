The Lonely Pig Latin Lady & the Land of Piglatinia

Pig Latin is a language that originates from Piglatinia. In Piglatinia, the population is primarily the indigenous Piglatinians.

Piglatinians are very friendly and enjoy hula hooping as their main pastime. They hold hula hooping in high regard and consider running a race without one to be quite disgraceful.

Piglatinians speak primarily Pig Latin, though some also speak español latín de cerdo (Spanish Pig Latin). Piglatinia is a beautiful and peaceful country — and the legend lives on through The Lonely Pig Latin Lady in America.
