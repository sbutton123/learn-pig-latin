What is Pig Latin?

Pig Latin originates from Piglatinia, a secret land of word flippers. It’s now a playful code in America, turning English words into fun phrases. Popular rumor says Thomas Jefferson used it, though it’s never confirmed!

How to Translate:

  • If a word starts with a vowel, add "yay" or "way" (e.g. "apple" → "appleyay").
  • If it starts with a consonant or cluster, move it to the end and add "ay" (e.g. "hello" → "ellohay").
  • Punctuation and capitalization stay the same.
